Python Camera App

This is created by Beena,Mandeep,Manpreet



Instructions:

  • Stop/Start--Toggle between stop or start live streaming
  • Capture--Take still-shot and save in the 'shots' directory
  • Grey--Toggle between grayscale and RGB output
  • Negative--Toggle between negative and RGB output
  • Face Only--Shows just your face if present(Toggle on/off)
  • Start/Stop Recording--Toggle between starting and stopping video recording
